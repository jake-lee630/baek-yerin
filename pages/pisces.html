Pisces (2022)

'Pisces' is the first singles album by Yerin Baek and was released on May 24, 2022. This album is meant to reminisce about childhood as we slowly enter adulthood. Just like we all received love, we must return the love back to the younger people that look up to us. This album is meant to comfort the sad thoughts about aging and create positivity around aging instead. The album has 3 tracks: "It was me", "Pisces" (title track), and "The loved one".
