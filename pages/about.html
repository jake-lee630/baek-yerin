About

Baek Yerin (Korean: 백예린), also know as Yerin Baek, is a South Korean singer songwriter. She is credited for writing and composing a majority of her songs, often touching upon personal topics and real life experiences.

Yerin Baek's discography includes studio albums, cover/remake albums, mini albums, single albums, digital singles, and OSTs. She also is a member of the indie rock band The Volunteers where they have released two albums.

Yerin Baek first made her debut under the South Korean duo 15& and made her solo debut with the mini album Frank in 2015. In 2018, Yerin Baek formed the indie rock band, The Volunteers, alongside Cloud (고형석), Jonny (곽민혁), and Chiheon (김치헌).

After 15& disbanded in 2019, Yerin Baek left JYP Entertainment and started her own independent label, Blue Vinyl. Soon after, Yerin Baek released her first studio album, Every Letter I Sent You and went on to win several awards along with 'Square (2017)' becoming the first song sung completely in English by a South Korean singer to top the charts.

In 2021, The Volunteers joined Blue Vinyl, and together launched their debut album, The Volunteers. Yerin Baek continued to release music, releasing tellusboutyourself, Love, Yerin, 'Pisces', and 'New Year'.

Yerin Baek also began to tour, starting with 'Turn on that Blue Vinyl'. She ventured into North America with the '2022 Yerin Baek North America Tour' before hosting 'Square' in Seoul, and finishing with the '2023 Yerin Baek Asia Pacific Tour.
