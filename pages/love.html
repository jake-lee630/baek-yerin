Our Love is Great (2019)

Our Love is Great is the second mini album by Yerin Baek. It was released on March 18, 2019, consisting of 7 tracks: "Merry And The Witch's Flower", "Maybe It's Not Our Fault" (title track), "Dear my blue", "Our love is great", "I Don't Know", "See You Again" and "Our Love is Great (2019 Version)".
