tellusboutyourself (2020)

tellusboutyourself is the sceond studio album by Yerin Baek. It was released on December 10, 2020, consisting of 14 tracks. Similarly to the first studio album (Every Letter I Sent You), this album also had double title tracks. Some tracks on this album include: "Lovegame", "Hall&Oates", "Hate You" (title track), "0415" (title track), "I'll be your family", and "Bubbles&Mushrooms".

a remix version was also released: tellusboutyourself (Remixes)
