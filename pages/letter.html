Every Letter I Sent You (2019)

Every Letter I Sent You is the first studio album by Yerin Baek. Released on December 10, 2019, the album had 2 CD's with 9 tracks each, along with two title tracks. Every Letter I Sent You was the first release under Yerin Baek's new label, Blue Vinyl. Tracks on the album include: "Popo (How deep is our love?)" (title track), "Bunny", "0310", "Datoom", "True Lover", "Square (2017)" (title track) and more.
