Love, Yerin (2021)

Love, Yerin is the first cover album and the third EP by Yerin Baek. It was released on September 10, 2021, with a double title track. The remake album covered artists such as The Black Skirts and NELL. In total, there are 6 tracks: "Whenever", "Antifreeze", "Go Back", "Why Me?" (title track), "Limit" and "A Walk" (title track).
